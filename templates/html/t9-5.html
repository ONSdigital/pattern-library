Datasets

Inflation and price indices datasets

4 results for datasets related to Inflation and price indices.

  • Index of Private Housing Rental Prices dataset: January to March 2015

    Released: 21 March 2015

    The Index of Private Housing Rental Prices (IPHRP) measures the change in price of renting residential property from private landlords.

  • House Price Index dataset: February 2015

    Released: 21 March 2015

    The House Price Index (HPI), is a monthly release that publishes figures for mix-adjusted average house prices and house price indices for the UK, its component countries and regions.

  • Consumer Price Inflation dataset: March 2015

    Released: 21 March 2015

    The Consumer Price Inflation is a monthly release that contains percentage changes and weights for the CPI, CPIH, RPI and RPIJ.

  • Producer Price Index dataset: March 2015

    Released: 21 March 2015

    Input and output producer price indices of materials and fuels purchased and output of manufacturing industry by broad sector.
